Keraleeyam

All-Day Dining Restaurant

With the most breathtaking views of the pristine Lake Ashtamudi, Keraleeyam offers you the soulful essence of God’s Own Country, Kerala. The ambience immerses you in the vibrant Malayalee culture, adorned with framed fishing boats and wooden panelling running across the ceiling. The space also features a miniature model of a 'Chundan Vallam' or a snake boat, a beautiful homage to the annual boat races traditional to the state. The decor might represent Kerala but the menu goes far beyond, serving you the finest Indian and international fare. Feast on delicacies like Golden Fried Stuffed Mushrooms, Crumb Fried Cottage Cheese, Thai Chicken Curry, Raviz Nirvana, Kanthari Paneer, Ashtamudi Fish Mango Curry, Venad Paal Konju, Malabari Biryani, and Mambazha Pulissery. With its serene vistas and breezes from the lake, Keraleeyam is the perfect spot from which to enjoy a lavish breakfast, a flavourful lunch, and an unforgettable dinner.
